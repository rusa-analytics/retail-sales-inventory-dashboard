Retail Sales & Inventory Intelligence Dashboard

An interactive retail analytics application built by RUSA Analytics to demonstrate how retailers can gain real-time visibility into sales, inventory, products, stores, and customer performance.

Live Demo

🔗 https://rusa-analytics.github.io/retail-sales-inventory-dashboard/

Business Challenge

Retail businesses often struggle with:

* Fragmented reporting across stores
* Limited visibility into inventory health
* Stock-outs impacting sales
* Difficulty tracking product profitability
* Lack of customer intelligence
* Slow decision-making due to manual reporting

Our Solution

RUSA Analytics developed a Retail Sales & Inventory Intelligence Dashboard providing a single view of business performance across multiple dimensions.

Dashboard Modules

Executive Overview

Track key KPIs including:

* Revenue
* Gross Profit
* Margin %
* Orders
* Units Sold
* Customer Metrics

Store Performance

Monitor:

* Revenue by Store
* Store Profitability
* Regional Performance
* Store Rankings

Inventory Intelligence

Analyze:

* Inventory Value
* Stock-Out Risks
* Reorder Alerts
* Inventory Health

Product Analytics

Evaluate:

* Top Products
* Product Profitability
* Category Performance
* Revenue Contribution

Customer Insights

Understand:

* Customer Segments
* Revenue by Demographics
* Loyalty Performance
* Customer Trends

Technologies Used

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript
* Chart.js
* GitHub Pages

Business Value

The dashboard demonstrates how retailers can:

* Improve visibility into business performance
* Reduce inventory risks
* Identify profitable products
* Improve store-level decision making
* Understand customer behaviour
* Accelerate executive reporting
